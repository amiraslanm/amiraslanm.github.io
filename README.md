# Amir Aslan Mosaferi

Mechanical Engineer | CFD Researcher | Thermal-Fluids | FSI / VIV / MHD | HVAC & Fire Protection Engineering

I am a mechanical engineer with research experience in computational fluid dynamics (CFD), fluid-structure interaction (FSI), vortex-induced vibration (VIV), magnetohydrodynamics (MHD), and convective heat transfer.

My M.Sc. research resulted in two peer-reviewed journal publications, including a first-author Q1 Elsevier paper in *International Communications in Heat and Mass Transfer*.

## Research Interests
- Computational Fluid Dynamics
- Fluid-Structure Interaction
- Vortex-Induced Vibration
- Heat Transfer
- Multiphase Flow and Aerosol Transport
- OpenFOAM / ANSYS Fluent / COMSOL
- Renewable Energy and Wind-Turbine Aerodynamics

## Selected Work
- CFD modelling of VIV suppression and heat transfer around a circular cylinder
- MHD-based flow-control analysis
- Plasma actuation review for Vertical Axis Wind Turbines
- HVAC, fire protection, smoke control, and NFPA-based engineering design
