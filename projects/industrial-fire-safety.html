FIRE ALARM · SPRINKLER

Integrated Fire Detection & Suppression for a Key Industrial Building

A generalized case study of fire alarm architecture, suppression and special detection for an industrial building. All identifiers, values and proprietary drawings have been removed.

ScopeDetection & suppression
Alarm architectureZone-module
Special detectionBeam · UV/IR

Design challenge

The multi-storey building required a coherent sprinkler and zone-module fire alarm strategy. Detector placement, zone division and equipment monitoring had to align with the building layout and product limitations.

Key decisions

Zone strategy

Logical division of floors and special spaces for faster identification and easier troubleshooting.

Beam Detection

Review of range, line of sight, obstructions and transmitter/receiver placement in high spaces.

UV/IR Flame Detection

Consideration of field of view, fuel characteristics, false alarm sources, power and monitoring requirements.

Suppression coordination

Review of riser, floor control assemblies, special spaces and alarm-system interfaces.
