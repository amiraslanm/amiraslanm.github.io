VIV Control Under Magnetic Field

A numerical study of vortex-induced vibration suppression and its impact on convective heat transfer.

DomainCFD · FSI · MHD
RoleFirst author
PublicationQ1 · Elsevier

Research question

Can a magnetic field suppress vortex-induced vibration of an elastically mounted cylinder, and how does that suppression affect convective heat transfer?

Numerical method

Flow and energy equations→Two-way FSI coupling→MHD Lorentz force→Validation and response analysis

Selected findings

  • Magnetic-field orientation had a decisive effect on vibration suppression.
  • Structural-motion suppression significantly altered vortex structure and force response.
  • Reduced vibration was accompanied by changes in convective heat transfer and must be evaluated jointly.
