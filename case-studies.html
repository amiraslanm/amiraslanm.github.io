CONFIDENTIAL-SAFE CASE STUDIES

Selected engineering case studies

These case studies summarize engineering thinking, coordination logic and design contributions without disclosing client names, project identifiers, drawings, exact design values, document numbers or sensitive project data.

CASE 01

Multi-Building Fire Protection Design Coordination

Challenge

Coordinating fire protection layouts across multiple building areas while keeping systems readable, constructible and aligned with other disciplines.

Approach

Reviewed sprinkler, hose/fire-box arrangements, zoning logic, riser coordination and drawing consistency against project requirements and approval needs.

Contribution

Prepared and revised design documentation, supported clash resolution and improved the clarity of fire protection deliverables.

CASE 02

Industrial Fire-Water and Foam System Review

Challenge

Reviewing fire-water demand, storage strategy and foam-related interfaces where multiple buildings and industrial hazards affect system logic.

Approach

Compared hydraulic requirements, pump/storage assumptions, foam equipment demand and distribution-network coordination in a confidential-safe review.

Contribution

Summarized design risks, clarified engineering assumptions and prepared decision-support notes for coordination and client communication.

CASE 03

Smoke Control and Stairwell Pressurization Support

Challenge

Coordinating mechanical exhaust, smoke clearance and stair pressurization requirements within architectural and mechanical constraints.

Approach

Reviewed airflow paths, fan/shaft logic, zoning assumptions, equipment placement and interface points with fire alarm and evacuation strategy.

Contribution

Supported system-level coordination and improved the connection between mechanical design and fire-safety objectives.
