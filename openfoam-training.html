WHAT I LEARNED

Key training notes for my portfolio

01

OpenFOAM case structure

Understanding the 0, constant and system folders, where dictionaries define fields, physics, mesh and run-time control.

02

blockMesh

Creating simple domains through blockMeshDict: vertices, blocks, edges, boundary patches, simpleGrading and checkMesh quality review.

03

snappyHexMesh

Meshing complex STL geometries with refinement, snapping, boundary layers and meshQualityControls to obtain body-fitted meshes.

04

codeStream

Using #codeStream to write C++ directly inside dictionaries for custom boundary or initial conditions without full solver development.

05

codedFixedValue

Defining velocity profiles such as parabolic or paraboloid inputs using patch face centers and simulation time access.

06

High-level programming

Introductory high-level BC programming: building a new library with .C/.H files, compiling with wmake and linking it to solvers.
